What is Study Bank?

Study Bank is a growing collection of competition and exam problems with detailed, step-by-step solutions. Each question is tagged by topic, subtopic, and difficulty level so you can focus your practice on exactly what you need.

This project is a work in progress. Questions, solutions, and features are being actively added and improved. Some solutions may be incomplete or contain errors — if you spot something, please send me an email or open a GitHub issue. If you have special requests for new features or cool ideas please also reach out.

Current Data

The database currently includes problems from:

  • CAP High School Physics Prize Exam — 1994 to 2025
  • 736 multiple-choice questions (Section A)
  • 92 free-response questions (Section B)
  • 828 total questions with solutions
